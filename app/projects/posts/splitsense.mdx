## What is it?

- All your expenses across Splitwise categorized and visualized cleanly.
- Utilizes Splitwise API for user data and OpenexchangeratesAPI for accurate currencies.
- Tech Stack: NextJS, TailwindCSS, Shadcn/UI, RadixUI, Vercel<br/><br/>
